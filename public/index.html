Our Story

We Believe in the Power of Every Woman

She Can Foundation was founded in 2015 with a single mission: to ensure that no woman is left behind because of poverty, lack of education, or social barriers. We work at the grassroots level across 8 Indian states.

From providing free digital literacy classes to connecting rural women with micro-enterprise funding, our programs are designed to create lasting, generational change.

  • Survivor-led and community-rooted programs
  • Transparent, accountable fund utilisation
  • Partnerships with government & corporate CSR

What We Do

Our Programs

Four pillars, one mission — a world where every woman thrives.

Education for Girls

We sponsor school fees, uniforms, and stationery for girls from below-poverty-line families, ensuring they never have to drop out.

  • Scholarships for Class 6–12
  • Bridge courses for dropouts
  • Digital literacy workshops
Learn more

Women's Healthcare

Free health camps, maternal care, and mental health support delivered directly to rural and peri-urban communities.

  • Monthly health camps
  • Maternal nutrition kits
  • Mental wellness circles
Learn more

Skills & Livelihoods

Tailoring, handicrafts, computer basics, and entrepreneurship training to help women become financially independent.

  • 3-month skill courses
  • Micro-enterprise funding
  • Market linkage support
Learn more

Legal Aid & Rights

Free legal counsel, awareness drives, and safe-space hotlines for survivors of domestic violence and workplace harassment.

  • Free legal consultations
  • 24/7 helpline support
  • Rights awareness camps
Learn more
